PLAYER STORIES

Behind every high score is a story. Here are a few tales from our local arcade legends.

LEVEL 1 STORY: RETRO PROPOSAL

The Leaderboard Proposal at Cabinet

Sarah and Mark met in 2024 during our street fighter tournament, standing side-by-side at Cabinet #4. Two years later, Mark secretly collaborated with our developer team to hack the cabinet's scoreboard.

"I thought I was just logging my new high score, but when I pressed enter, the screen read: SARAH WILL YOU MARRY ME? The whole bar went crazy. We're hosting our wedding party here next summer!"

A story that went viral in our local gaming community, proving that co-op play is always better than solo runs.

LEVEL 2 STORY: PAC-MARATHON

The 36-Hour Pac-Man Marathon Support

When Dave "PacLord" Henderson announced he was going to break his personal endurance score on our 1980 Pac-Man cabinet, the whole community rallied. Our kitchen staff stayed open all night, keeping him supplied with customized mini sliders and cold brew.

"I couldn't take my hands off the stick. The chef would literally feed me sliders and hold a straw to my mouth between levels. It was a true team effort!"

Dave hit a record score of 3,333,360 points before finally calling it a night. The cabinet now bears a custom gold-plated plaque in his honor.
