THE NEON ARCADE SANCTUARY

Enter the grid. We are a vintage gaming sanctuary, a craft beverage laboratory, and a pixel-drenched social portal located in the heart of the Neon District.

ESTABLISHED 198X / 202X

THE FIRST COIN INSERTED

It started with a single, flickering *Pac-Man* cabinet rescued from a damp warehouse basement. Three retro gaming enthusiasts spent months restoring its CRT monitor, recapping the chassis, and repairing its wooden joints.

That rescue grew into a collection, and that collection grew into a dream: a cyberpunk social hub where vintage gaming culture fuses with premium craft cocktails. We rebuilt this grid so you could play games the way they were meant to be played—on genuine glass screens with mechanical joysticks.

PRESERVATION HUB

THE RESTORATION LAB

How we maintain authentic cathode-ray screen visuals and retro hardware inputs.

CRT Recapping

We source authentic vintage capacitors to rebuild monitor boards, correcting colors, focus, and scanning lines for true 240p gaming warmth.

Joystick Calibration

Standard buttons are swapped for authentic Japanese Sanwa and Seimitsu joysticks, ensuring pixel-perfect combat responsiveness.

ROM Preservation

Original circuit boards are kept clean and functional. We preserve game chips to safeguard gaming history and high-score boards.
