STAGE 01 - PIXEL LEGENDS

Classic Arcade Cabinets

Step back into the golden era of pixel supremacy. Our arcade features fully restored, original upright wood cabinets from the 1980s and 90s. Experience the authentic weight of arcade joystick microswitches, vertical CRT shadow-mask monitors, and standard cabinet side art. Play legends like retro Pac-Man, original Galaga, Donkey Kong, and Space Invaders.

Display Rig 19\" Raster CRT Shadow-Mask Monitor
Control Board Authentic 4-Way Joysticks & Concave Buttons
Audio Out Mono Retro Cabinet Speaker with Subwoofer
STAGE 02 - CARTRIDGE LOUNGE

Retro Console Games

Rest your feet in our comfortable retro lounge. We have set up vintage wood-grain CRT televisions connected to fully restored vintage home consoles. Select a cartridge from our physical library, blow on the contacts, and snap it in. Systems include the NES, SNES, Sega Genesis, and Atari 2600.

Hardware Restored Original NES, SNES & Sega Genesis
Monitor 20\" Sony Trinitron CRT Color Television
Amenities Velvet Couches, Retro Beanbags, and Cartridge
STAGE 03 - STEEL BALL FURY

Pinball Machines

Feel the mechanical feedback and hear the chime reels ring. We feature a collection of fully restored solid-state and mechanical pinball machines from Bally, Williams, and Stern. Bright retrofitted LED bulbs, zero-friction playfields, and sensitive tilt plumb-bobs provide an authentic arcade flipper experience.

Table Build Tempered Playfield Glass, High-Flux Flippers
Tilt System Plumb Bob Tilt Sensor with Warning LED Indicators
Audio Original Chime Box Reels & Multi-Speaker Digital
STAGE 04 - NITRO SPEEDWAY

Racing Games

Get behind the wheel and shift into high gear. Our linked racing cockpits put you in the driver's seat with physical force-feedback steering wheels, active steel throttle/brake assemblies, and four-speed manual shifters. Challenge your friends to head-to-head tracks in Daytona USA, OutRun, and Hydro Thunder.

Cabinet Type Linked Sit-Down Cockpits with Stereo Headrests
Controls Force-Feedback Wheel, H-Pattern Shifter, Steel
Display Twin 32\" High-Resolution CRT Displays
STAGE 05 - FIGHT CLUB

Fighting Games

Head-to-head rivalry, zero-latency inputs, and quarter-circle punches. Our fighting cabinet lineup utilizes face-to-face configurations (preventing screen-peeking) and premium Japanese Sanwa arcade components. Prove your combos and execution in Street Fighter II: Turbo, Mortal Kombat II, and Tekken 3.

Lever / Button Sanwa JLF Joysticks & OBSF-30 Convex Buttons
Cabinet Build Face-to-Face Dual Cabinet Link System
Input latency Zero-Latency PCB Wiring for Competitive Play
STAGE 06 - FOUR-PLAYER CO-OP

Multiplayer Arcade Games

Gather your squad and conquer the streets together. Our large-format multiplayer cabinets support up to 4 simultaneous players. Work together to rescue the city and defeat high-difficulty bosses. Rigs are preloaded with Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, X-Men, and The Simpsons Arcade Game.

Control Layout Wide-Angle 4-Player simultaneous Control Deck
Cabinet Width 42\" Custom Hardwood Cabinet Chassis
Display 37\" High-Definition Presentation Monitor
